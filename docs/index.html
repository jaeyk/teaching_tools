ClassKit

Run cold calls, breakout groups, preference groups, and peer matches in your browser.

ClassKit is a lightweight suite of browser-based classroom utilities. Upload a roster, choose your options, and generate reproducible student samples, breakout groups, preference-based teams, and peer-review matches. No installation required.

Choose a tool

Pick the tab that fits your classroom moment. Uploads stay in your browser.

Cold calling

Upload a two-column roster (name,excused) to sample students while honoring opt-outs. Each click reshuffles unless you supply an optional seed.

Breakout groups

Upload a single-column list of names to split students into balanced teams by target group size or number of teams.

Peer review matches

Upload individual or group names to create a round-robin set of review partners. Each participant reviews one other and is reviewed once.
